How To Crush Locked Candies

From "Level 36" onwards, you will encounter locked candies. These are regular candies, but are "locked." You cannot move them or swap with other candies unless you make the right moves in the game. Look at the possible match of 3 green candies. Swapping the green candy with the red one will not unlock the purple locked candy. To get rid of these locks, you need to swap the locked candies with other same-colored candies. Now, look at the possible match of orange candies. Swapping the orange candy with the red one will unlock the locked orange candy. However, this move will not eliminate this orange candy with the other two orange candies. This means that you need to match it one more time.
